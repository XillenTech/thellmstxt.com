Smartcat Platform Overview
-------------------------
Smartcat (https://www.smartcat.com/) is an all-in-one AI translation and localization platform for enterprises, L&D, and global teams. It offers AI-powered translation, a marketplace of 500,000+ linguists, workflow automation, integrations, and tools for managing multilingual content at scale.
